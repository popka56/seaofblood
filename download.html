Requirements

In order to play Sea of Blood you need a Windows PC, a broadband internet connection (for online play) and a minimum of 210MB of space on your hard drive. For more detailed requirements, see the Steam store page. Please note, in older versions of the game, sometimes the game will just be a black screen on first startup. If this happens, just restarting the game should resolve it.
